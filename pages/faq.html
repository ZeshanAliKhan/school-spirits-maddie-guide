School Spirits Maddie FAQ

Who killed Maddie in School Spirits?

The shortest honest answer is that nobody killed her in the clean, ordinary way the question suggests. The real crisis is Janet taking Maddie’s body.

Did Janet kill Maddie?

Not in the usual crime-drama sense. Janet possesses Maddie’s body, which causes the catastrophe, but the show frames it as possession and displacement rather than a neat murder confession.

Why is Mr. Martin still important?

Because he knows more than he admits and helps keep the truth buried. Even without being a simple physical killer, he is central to the damage.

Why does the twist feel so different from a normal reveal?

Because the show changes the genre logic. Maddie is not just a ghost solving her own murder. She is a displaced soul trying to understand why her life was stolen.
